What's Included

●
Ad Strategy & Setup
Facebook, Instagram, and Google ad campaigns built from scratch, audience targeting, creative direction, copy, and budget structure. We set up the campaigns that actually match your offer to your buyer.
●
Sales Funnels & Landing Pages
High-converting landing pages and multi-step funnels that move leads from cold traffic to booked call or purchase. Built with your offer, your audience, and your conversion goal in mind.
●
Email & SMS Nurture Sequences
Automated sequences that follow up with leads at the right time, with the right message. Open a lead, don't close them immediately, follow up over 7-14 days and let the sequence do the work.
●
Booking Automation
Seamless flow from ad click to booked call, form submission triggers confirmation, calendar invite, prep email, and reminder sequence. No manual scheduling. No dropped balls.
●
Lead Magnets & Opt-In Flows
Build your list with a valuable freebie, checklist, or guide, then drop leads into a nurture sequence. Works alongside paid traffic or as a standalone organic strategy.
●
Retargeting Setup
Pixel setup and retargeting audiences for website visitors, video viewers, and form openers. Most people don't convert on the first touch, retargeting brings them back.
